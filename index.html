Tap

At Santa Clara there's a lack of community between students. Students from different culture tend not to mix with other cultures especially in the graduate program. In fact, there's no reunion held for graduate students because of the lack of community. There's also a barrier between students from different departments. Students from different departments of the college are unlikely to ever cross paths. TAP is a niche social media app that is designed for the college students with the goal of gettting rid of all barries that exist. It is an app connects students through similar interest and also randomly through weekly coffee chats. It is the first step towards building a large cohesive community between students.
